# 11 — Research Sources

Authoritative references consulted for the scoring/format specs (doc 04/05). Cite these
when implementing; re-verify editions at build time (rules change — e.g. FIFA changed its
tiebreaker order for 2026).

## Football
- [FIFA World Cup 2026 — groups, qualification & tie-breakers](https://www.fifa.com/en/tournaments/mens/worldcup/canadamexicousa2026/articles/groups-how-teams-qualify-tie-breakers) — head-to-head-first cascade (2026 change, aligned with UEFA)
- [FOX Sports — 2026 group stage tiebreakers & third-place advancement](https://www.foxsports.com/stories/soccer/fifa-world-cup-group-stage-third-place-tiebreakers)
- [JudgeMate — World Cup 2026 tiebreakers explained](https://www.judgemate.com/en/guides/world-cup-2026-group-stage-tiebreakers-explained) — incl. fair-play points scale

## Cricket
- [ESPNcricinfo — Net Run Rate explained](https://www.espncricinfo.com/ci/content/page/429305.html) — NRR formula, all-out full-quota rule
- [CricHeroes — NRR calculator methodology](https://blog.cricheroes.com/net-run-rate-calculator/) — decimal-overs conversion, worked examples
- [Wikipedia — Duckworth–Lewis–Stern method](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Duckworth%E2%80%93Lewis%E2%80%93Stern_method) — resource-percentage model, Standard vs Professional edition
- [arXiv 2106.00175 — DLS comparison with ML approaches](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2106.00175) — resource table background
- ICC Playing Conditions (icc-cricket.com) — points/NR conventions, super over; fetch current edition when implementing PROMPT-05
