title: Presentation mode
description: A no-login kiosk URL — point any TV or projector at your competition and let it run.
order: 8
---

Every public competition and division has a **Present** page: a full-screen, auto-rotating board of standings, fixtures, live scores and the bracket. No login, no setup — open the URL on the venue TV (or cast it) and walk away.

Find **Present ▸** next to the share controls on your public competition or division page, or go straight to `/shared/<club>/<competition>/present` (add `/<division>` for one division). While matches are **in play**, the live slide pins itself into the rotation — the screen returns to the action every other slide.

The board wears your public branding. It refreshes itself; results and tables appear as they land. For the login-side equivalent inside your console (with realtime push on Pro), see [the slideshow](/help/sharing/slideshow).
