The public dashboard is your competition's home for everyone who isn't running it: players checking when they're on, parents following scores, the league watching the table.

## What's on it

- **Competition page** — hero with your branding, description, live-now strip, division cards, sponsors, and *Register now* while registration is open.
- **Division pages** — schedule (with live "scorebug" rows), standings, brackets and entrants as tabs; a champion banner when it's decided.
- **Match pages** — live score detail, updating without a refresh.
- **Calendar feeds** — every division has an iCal link; subscribe once, matches appear in any calendar app.

## Your brand on it

Your **org logo** sits on the public tree on every plan, free included. The **brand colour** and competition banner that theme the rest of the pages are a **Pro** feature. Either way, the preview images that appear when links are shared to WhatsApp or social media carry the same look.

## Making it findable

Visibility is the switch ([details](/help/sharing/visibility)); the [QR poster](/help/getting-started/share-your-dashboard) bridges the venue to the page; the [slideshow](/help/sharing/slideshow) puts it on the venue TV.
