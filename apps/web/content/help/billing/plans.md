## Community — free forever

Run real competitions, free: 2 active competitions, 3 team members, 16 entrants per division, 1 public dashboard, full scoring and scheduling. When something is finished, complete or archive it to free the slot.

## Pro

Everything unlimited, plus the organiser toolkit: your branding on public pages and share images, entry fees via Stripe, spreadsheet imports and exports, clubs, player stats, advanced formats, API keys and embeds. Starts with a 14-day trial, no card required.

## Event Pass

One-time upgrade for a single competition — every division inside it gets Pro features for that event's lifetime, without a subscription. Right for the annual tournament that doesn't justify a year of Pro. It doesn't carry to next year's edition.

## Common questions

**What happens to my data if I stop paying?** Nothing is deleted, ever — see [downgrading](/help/billing/downgrade).

**Where are receipts?** Settings → Billing → Manage billing opens your Stripe portal with every invoice.
