## Community — free forever

Run real competitions, free: 2 active competitions, 3 team members, 16 entrants per division, 1 public dashboard, full scoring and scheduling, and 1 official per fixture. When something is finished, complete or archive it to free the slot.

## Event Pass — $29 one-time

One-time upgrade for a single competition — every division inside it gets Pro features for that event's lifetime, without a subscription. Right for the annual tournament that doesn't justify a year of Pro. It doesn't carry to next year's edition.

## Pro — $19/month

Everything unlimited, plus the organiser toolkit: your branding on public pages and share images, entry fees via Stripe, spreadsheet imports and exports, clubs, player stats, advanced formats, read-only API keys and embeds (score & manage keys need Pro Plus). Starts with a 14-day trial, no card required — one trial per organisation; if you come back to Pro later, billing starts from day one.

## Pro Plus — $39/month

Everything in Pro, plus unlimited seats, scorers and clubs, a 1% platform fee on entry fees (instead of Pro's flat rate), AI-assisted scheduling, automatic officials assignment, a write-scoped API key for integrations, and priority support. Built for platforms, leagues and federations running many competitions at once. The pricing page reveals the Pro Plus card behind a "Show Pro Plus" button below the main three offers — it's the same 4-plan comparison table underneath either way.

## Common questions

**What happens to my data if I stop paying?** Nothing is deleted, ever — see [downgrading](/help/billing/downgrade).

**Where are receipts?** Every invoice is emailed to your billing address when it's charged. Your plan, payment card, billing address and VAT details are all managed in **Settings → Billing** — no external portal.
