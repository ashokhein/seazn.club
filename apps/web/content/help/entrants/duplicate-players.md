The same person often ends up in the directory twice — typed in by hand one season, imported from a spreadsheet the next. Merging fixes that without touching any results.

## How to merge

In **Directory → Players**, find the entry you want to get rid of and choose **merge…**, then pick the player to keep. Everything attached to the duplicate moves to the kept player:

- **Team memberships** — every entrant roster the duplicate was on.
- **Fixture lineups** — past selections stay intact under the kept name.
- **Sport profiles** — position and attribute profiles per sport.

Where both entries were already on the same team, fixture or sport profile, the kept player's entry wins and the duplicate's copy is dropped. The duplicate is then deleted.

## What to check first

- **Keep the right one.** The kept player's name, date of birth, photo and consent settings are what remain — merge the sparse entry *into* the complete one.
- **It can't be undone.** There's no unmerge; if two entries turn out to be different people after all, you'll be re-adding one by hand.
- **Results are safe.** Match results and score history are never affected by a merge.

## Common questions

**Why do I have duplicates at all?** Usually one import and one manual add with slightly different spellings. Skimming the players list alphabetically before a new season starts catches most of them.
