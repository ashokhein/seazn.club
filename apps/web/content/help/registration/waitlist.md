Set a **capacity** on registration and the division can never overfill. Entry number *capacity + 1* is offered the **waitlist** instead of a rejection.

## How it works

- Waitlisted players get a ticket like everyone else, marked **WAITLIST**, with their live status at their reference link.
- When a confirmed entry withdraws, the **first waitlisted entry promotes automatically** — they're notified by email, and if the division has an entry fee, payment is collected at promotion (never while waiting).
- You can also promote manually from the registrations tab, in any order.

## Common questions

**Do waitlisted players pay?** No — money is only taken when a real spot is theirs.

**Can I see the queue order?** Yes — the registrations tab shows waitlisted entries oldest-first; that's the automatic promotion order.

**Can I close the waitlist?** Closing registration stops new entries entirely; the existing queue keeps promoting on withdrawals until you withdraw the waitlisted entries too.
