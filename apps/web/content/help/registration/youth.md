title: Youth divisions and privacy
description: Under-18 divisions get guardian consent at registration and shortened names on public pages.

---

Set an under-age eligibility rule (U16, U12…) on a division and it is automatically flagged as a **youth division**. Two protections switch on:

## Guardian consent

The registration form adds a consent step: a parent or guardian ticks the box and gives their name. Entries without it can't be submitted.

## Name privacy on public pages

Public dashboards, standings and share images show youth players as **first name + initial** ("Maya K"), never full names. Your organiser console and exports keep full names — the shortening applies only to what strangers can see.

You can change the display style per division, and making a youth division public asks you to confirm you hold guardian consent for the players it will show.

## Common questions

**We're a school — can we hide names entirely?** Keep the division Private and share the dashboard link only with your community; link-only pages are not indexed by search engines.

**Does the flag ever set itself wrongly?** It derives from your eligibility rules and you can override it in the division settings.
