Scoring opens when you **start** the division ([why starting locks setup](/help/divisions/lifecycle)). From then on, every fixture has a score pad tuned to its sport: sets and games for racquet sports ([tennis scores like tennis](/help/scoring/tennis) — deuce, tie-breaks and all), overs and wickets for cricket, [periods, penalties and shoot-outs for the hockeys](/help/scoring/hockey), a plain score pad for everything else.

## The flow

1. Open the fixture (from the fixtures tab, the board, or *My matches* for scorers).
2. **Start** the match, enter points as they happen — or just the final score at the end.
3. **Finalize** when it's done. Standings, progression and public dashboards update on every entry.

## Corrections

Results are an append-only ledger: fixing a mistake adds a correction rather than erasing history, so a disputed scoreline can always be reconstructed. Finalized matches are locked; an admin can void a wrongly-entered match before finalization.

## Common questions

**Two people scored the same match at once?** The second entry is rejected and refreshed rather than silently overwriting — see [score conflicts](/help/scoring/conflicts).

**Offline venue?** Scores queue on the device and send when signal returns.
