A **device link** turns any phone or tablet into a scoreboard for exactly one fixture — no account, no app install.

## Mint one

On a fixture, choose **Device link**. You get a link (and QR) to hand to whoever is at the court. It works immediately.

## What the holder can do

Score that one match. Nothing else — they can't finalize it, can't see other fixtures, can't touch settings. If they enter something wrong, they can void their own last entry before the match is finalized.

## Lifetime and revocation

- Expires automatically at the **end of the fixture's local day**.
- Minting a new link for the same fixture revokes the previous one.
- Revoke any time from the fixture — the device is signed out on its next tap.

## Common questions

**Scorer role vs device link?** Scorers are teammates with accounts and standing assignments across many matches. Device links are day-of, one match, zero setup — made for the parent volunteering at Court 3.
