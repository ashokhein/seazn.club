## Pinning one match

Pin a match (📌 → 🔒 on its card) and every automatic pass — Auto-schedule, Re-flow remaining, Clear slots — leaves it exactly where it is. Use it for the final that must be at 3pm, or the match your streamers are set up for.

You can still drag a pinned match yourself; it stays pinned at its new slot.

## Freezing everything

To stop **all** edits — yours included — freeze the whole schedule from the History panel. A frozen schedule is read-only until unfrozen; useful once the timetable is printed and taped to the wall.

## Common questions

**Does pinning stop score entry?** No — pins and freezes are about the timetable, never about results.

**Who can unfreeze?** Anyone who can edit the division; the freeze and unfreeze both land in the division history with who did it.
