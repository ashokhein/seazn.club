## Archive

Archiving hides a division from the console, the public site and your plan quota — but keeps every result. Restore it any time (restoring re-checks your plan's division quota).

Use it for: last season's divisions cluttering the console, or a division created by mistake **after** matches were played.

## Delete

Delete permanently removes a division — allowed only while it has **no recorded play**. Once results exist you'll be offered archive instead: results are a shared record (players point at them), so they never silently vanish.

An archived division can be purged permanently after 30 days in the archive — a deliberate cool-off, not a second delete button.

## Common questions

**Does archiving free a Community slot?** Yes — archived divisions don't count against limits; that's often the painless answer to "over quota after downgrade".

**Where do I find these?** The division's **Settings tab → Danger zone**.

**Who can archive or delete?** Owners and admins; both actions require typing the division's name to confirm and land in the org audit trail.
