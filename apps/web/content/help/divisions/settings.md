Every division has a **Settings tab** (visible to editors) beside Entrants, Fixtures, Standings and Stats. It groups four sections:

- **General** — rename the division and upload a **card logo**. The logo shows on the division's card in your console; without one the card wears a monogram in the division's colour. Remove the logo any time to go back to the monogram.
- **Format** — choose the competition structure (League, Knockout, Groups + Knockout, Swiss…) with its knobs (top-N advance, group count, rounds), plus the match rules (variant, standings points, per-sport rules) — all editable **until fixtures are generated**. Changing the structure rebuilds the division's stages. Once any stage has fixtures the format locks for good ("Format is locked — fixtures exist"); delete the stages first if you truly must change it. The **Entrants** section is the one exception — entrant settings stay editable after fixtures exist. Edits are validated by the sport's own rules before saving.
- **Sharing & embed** — the copy-paste iframe snippet for your website ([details](/help/sharing/embeds)).
- **Danger zone** — [archive or delete](/help/divisions/archive) the division.

## Common questions

**Why can't I change the format?** Fixtures exist — a schedule (and any results) were built on the old format. On the **Fixtures tab**, delete the stages first (last stage down; a plain League has just the one), then the Format section unlocks. If a stage already has **played** results — anything in play, decided or finalized — it can't be deleted; archive the division and start a fresh one instead.

**Who sees the Settings tab?** Owners, admins and editors of the competition. Viewers never see it.
