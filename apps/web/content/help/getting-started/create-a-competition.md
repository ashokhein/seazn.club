A **competition** is the event itself: "Summer League 2026", "Spring Smash". It holds one or more divisions, its dates, a description and its own public dashboard.

## Create one

1. From your dashboard press **New competition**.
2. Give it a name — the web address is derived from it automatically.
3. Add dates and a description. The description supports headings, images, links and a call-to-action button, and shows on your public page exactly as the Preview tab shows it.

## Visibility

Every competition starts **Private** (only your team sees it). Switch to **Link only** to share with anyone who has the URL, or **Public** to be findable on search engines and our Discover page. You can change this at any time in the competition's settings.

## Common questions

**Free plan limits?** Community orgs can run 2 active competitions at a time. Finished ones can be marked completed or archived to free a slot.
