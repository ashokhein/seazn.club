Every competition has a **public dashboard**: live scores, schedules, standings and entrants, readable on any phone with no account.

## Share it

1. Set the competition's visibility to **Link only** or **Public** ([what these mean](/help/sharing/visibility)).
2. Copy the link from the sharing panel — or share straight to WhatsApp with one tap.
3. Print the **QR PDF** and stick it at the venue; a scan opens the live dashboard.

## At the venue

Point a TV or projector at the [slideshow](/help/sharing/slideshow) — it cycles standings and matchups by itself and stays current.

## On your own website

Pro orgs can [embed](/help/sharing/embeds) live standings, schedules or brackets as an iframe that keeps itself in sync.

That's the whole loop: set up → enter → score → share. Everything else in this help centre is depth on those four steps.
