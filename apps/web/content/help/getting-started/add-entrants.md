Entrants are who competes — the teams, players or pairs in a division.

## Four ways in

- **Type them** — the entrants tab takes one name per line; paste a whole list at once.
- **Import a spreadsheet** — upload CSV or Excel; you get a dry-run preview of exactly what will be created before anything is written.
- **Open registration** — let players sign themselves up from your public page, with optional entry fees, custom questions and a waitlist. See [Open registration](/help/registration/open-registration).
- **Through the API** — for integrations, see the [developer docs](/developers).

## Seeds and withdrawals

Set seeds on the entrants tab to control bracket placement. If someone drops out after fixtures exist, use **Withdraw** — the schedule repairs itself with walkovers or expunged results depending on how far the division has run ([details](/help/entrants/withdrawals)).
